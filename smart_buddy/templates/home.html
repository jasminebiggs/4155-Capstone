Welcome to SmartBuddy

SmartBuddy is a platform designed to help UNC Charlotte students find compatible study partners based on shared academic interests, study styles, and availability. Whether you prefer quiet solo sessions or collaborative group environments, SmartBuddy makes it easy to connect with others who match your study needs. Start by creating your profile and exploring matched users today.

About Us

Smart Buddy is your personalized study partner matching system. Our goal is to help you connect with other students who share your academic focus and preferred study style. Together, you can boost productivity, stay accountable, and make studying more enjoyable!
